Welcome to valhalla


We are a small, friendly tabletop Gaming group based in Letchworth Garden City. We primarily play games made by Games workshop including Warhammer 40K, Age of Sigmar, Underworlds and many more. We believe gaming should be fun so we take great effort to create a light-hearted and friendly atmosphere that is welcoming to new and veteran players alike, All Skill levels are welcome, We can accommodate small beginner games as well as large all-out battles. We also have a very active hobbying community and we run painting competitions and have a monthly showcase of models we have painted on discord. We recommend joining our active Discord server to come chat with us and hang out. Your first gaming session is free and if you decide to come back it's only Â£2 per evening, a tuck shop is also available for refreshments. Use the links at the top of the page to visit our Facebook group and join our discord server or head on down to Beowulf gym on friday night , Use the find us page to see where we are. Happy Gaming!

What We Play


We play most game systems made by Games Workshop but we are always willing to try new games so feel free to ask! We have a large amount of Tables, Gaming matts and scenary avaliable so we can accommodate most games. If you would like to try a new game, play a beginners game or just want help arranging a match for your first visit to the club, please let us know. A few examples of the games we play:

  • Warhammer 40k
  • Age Of Sigmar
  • Warhammer Underworlds
  • Kill Team
  • WarCry
  • Adeptus Titanicus
  • BloodBowl
